# Merchant Center And Feed

sklep.ekologus.pl requires product/feed intelligence from the beginning.

Diagnostics:

- account status,
- product inventory,
- feed data source status,
- product input status,
- disapprovals,
- missing required fields,
- weak titles/descriptions,
- category gaps,
- PMax retail readiness,
- Shopping Ads readiness.

Feed changes must preserve factual product attributes and validate before apply.
